Experiment 2 - Living Impressions

Description

This experient displays generative impression of this image:

[source]

Technical

Color
The sky, grass, trees, cloud, and ducks were estimated based on testing different colors on the color wheel tool via Google.

Primitives
The trees are rendered with `triangle` primitives.
The duck are rendered with various `triangle` and `ellipse` primatives.

Polygons
The riverbank and grass land are two single randomly generated polygon using `vertex` calls.

Composition
Different noise values lead to differently shaped river bank and grass land. The trees are positioned along the grass land vertexes.
The trees sizes, cloud color, cloud speed, and duck speed are generated randomly.

Life
The position of cloud and ducks are animated over time as if to simulate the view from ducks swimming and clouds moving.

Reflection

Overall, the experiment was easier than expected. However, there were some hiccups along the way. One hiccup that took the longest to solve was why the trees kept "moving," but later I discovered that since the trees were 'drawn' every frame, the size was changing every frame. Other than that, the experiment was a success and the shapes turned out pretty good.
* ChatGPT was a major help throughout this project.
