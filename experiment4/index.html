Description

This experiemnt displays procedural generation to create infinite worlds.
The theme of world 1 is a duck in the plains who can enjoy as much bread as it wants.
The theme of world 2 is a duck in space and it's goal is to explore the cosmos.
The theme of world 3 is a duck lost at sea, simulating Moby Duck.

Inspirations:
Duck 1 & 2 Duck 3 Garbage Planet Bread
